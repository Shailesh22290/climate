ASL380: Climate Modeling. 3-0-0-3 | This course covers the basics of the climate system and provides an introduction to climate modeling. Students will explore the conservation of mass, momentum, and energy, alongside horizontal and vertical energy transfer, moist processes, and wave processes.

Key Topics

  • • Climate system & conservation laws
  • • Numerical modeling & parameterization
  • • Model simulation, tuning & validation
  • • Atmospheric & wave processes

Learning Approach

The course uses case studies to connect theory with practice. Students will conduct phenomenological and process studies, such as modeling the influence of mountains on circulation or the climate effects of doubling CO2.
