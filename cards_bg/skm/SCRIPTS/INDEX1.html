Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, India.

About

Saroj Kanta Mishra is the YES Foundation Chair Professor of Climate Modeling, the Principal Investigator of the DST Centre of Excellence, and a Professor in the Centre for Atmospheric Sciences at IIT Delhi. Before joining IIT Delhi in 2012, Prof. Mishra served at Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology, India, University of Colorado at Boulder, and National Centre for Atmospheric Research USA. He is a graduate in Mechanical Engineering from UCE, Burla and holds a Ph. D. in Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences from IISc, Bangalore. Prof. Mishra is a Climate Modeller with interests in the broad topics of Atmospheric and Climate Sciences. He has published extensively in these research areas. He served (2010-2015) as an Associate Editor of the Asia-Pacific Journal of Atmospheric Sciences, an international journal co-published by the Korean Meteorological Society and Springer.
